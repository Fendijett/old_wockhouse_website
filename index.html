Important Tips

  • Identify the primary purpose of the server: Is it for hosting websites, managing databases, file storage, or something else?
  • Consider what level of security is required for your data and applications.
  • Have a clear understanding of your budget for both initial setup and ongoing maintenance.
  • Understand the purpose of the website: Is it for e-commerce, lead generation, branding, or something else?
  • Have your content ready, including text, images, videos, and other media that will be used on the website.
  • Make a list of essential features you need, such as e-commerce capabilities, contact forms, booking systems, etc.
